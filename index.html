How to Play

  • Every gameweek a prediction form is posted in Discord. Predict the final score of every match on that week's coupon.
  • Pick a nickname and reuse the exact same one every week. It's how your points add up all season — and it keeps you anonymous on this table.
  • Deadline: predictions close at the first kickoff of the gameweek. Miss it and you sit that week out.
  • On the form you also nominate your Banker and choose whether to play your Joker (see rules below).

Scoring & House Rules

You predict a scoreline; the result is read from it: 1 home win · X draw · 3 away win.
  • +1 — correct outcome (your 1 / X / 3 matches the real result)
  • +3 — exact scoreline (the outcome point plus a +2 bonus)
  • 0 — wrong outcome
  • 🏦 Banker — the one match you nominate each week scores double
  • 🃏 Joker — play it to double your entire gameweek (limited uses per season — time it well)
Example: you predict Man Utd 1–3 Man City (an away win, “3”). Finishes 0–2 → still an away win → +1. Finishes exactly 1–3 → +3. Finishes 2–1 → wrong → 0.

Postponed matches score nothing until they're played, then get counted automatically whenever they happen. Most points at the end of the season wins the league. 🏆
